The Heart of Kottayam's High Ranges

Nestled in the lush landscapes of the Kottayam district in Kerala, Teekoy is more than just a village; it's an experience. Acting as the primary entry point to the breathtaking Vagamon hills, every traveler passing through is treated to rolling meadows, serene tea gardens, and misty mornings.

Whether you're seeking a quiet retreat into nature or an adventurous hike through pine forests and waterfalls, your journey begins here in Teekoy.

Explore Teekoy

Top attractions in and around Teekoy. Discover the hidden gems of the region.

Vagamon

The famous hill station known for its rolling meadows, pine forests, and misty climate, just a short drive from Teekoy.

Illikkal Kallu

A majestic and unique rock formation atop a lush green mountain, offering epic panoramic views of the landscape.

Marmala Waterfalls

A beautiful cascading waterfall hidden deep inside a lush green tropical forest. Perfect for nature lovers.

Ayyampara

A vast flat rocky terrain on top of a hill featuring a small temple and stunning sunset views over the valleys.

Kattikayam Waterfalls

A scenic, somewhat hidden waterfall tumbling down rocky terrains, surrounded by dense green foliage.

Kadavupuzha Aruvi Waterfalls

A beautiful, pristine stream and waterfall area, offering a refreshing retreat into nature.

Ilaveezhapoonchira (24 km)

Located 24 km away, this beautiful valley is surrounded by large hills with misty and dramatic atmospheres.

Explore Vagamon

Just a short drive from Teekoy, discover the wonders of the high ranges.

Vagamon Meadows

Endless rolling hills covered in velvety green grass. The perfect spot for a peaceful picnic or a quiet sunset.

Pine Forest

A majestic man-made forest. Walk through the tall pines as sunlight filters through the misty canopy.

Kurisumala

A serene spiritual site located on a hill, offering panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and tea estates.

Thangal Para

A massive spherical rock formation perched on a hill, providing stunning, sweeping views of the Vagamon landscape.
